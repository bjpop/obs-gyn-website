A Gynaecologist is a doctor specializing in the medical and surgical management of disorders of the female reproductive system and related structures.

An Obstetrician is a doctor specializing in management of pregnancy and birth including complicated births such as Caesarean sections and instrumental deliveries (forceps and vacuum).

As a Specialist Obstetrician and Gynaecologist, Dr McCallum provides care in both fields in both the private and public health care systems.
